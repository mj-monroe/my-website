Memes of the Early 2000s

In the early 2000s, memes became a widespread form of internet humor. Moms and teens alike were posting and viewing them on Facebook (The only large social media platform at the time). The formula for a perfect meme comprised of a funny image with a bold white text that referenced something everyone could understand. There were also the classic meme faces that were seen just about everywhere
